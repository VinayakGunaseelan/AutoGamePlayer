Launch recorder.py
        ↓
Small GUI appears (outside game window)
        ↓
Press start button
        ↓
3 second countdown on GUI
        ↓
Recording begins:
├── Screenshot every 33ms (30fps)
├── Log every key press/release + timestamp
└── Log mouse x/y delta + timestamp
        ↓
Press DELETE key
        ↓
Recording stops
        ↓
Save to data/recordings/session_XXX/
├── frames/     → frame_001.png, frame_002.png...
└── inputs.csv  → timestamp, keys, mouse_x, mouse_y
